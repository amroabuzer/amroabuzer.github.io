Presymptomatic Covid Prediction

Introduction

This was a hackathon project in the TUM.ai makethon. We explored different methods to detect, presymptomatically, coronavirus in patients based on heart rate aggregated by fitness bands. We were the finalists out of 38 participating teams and pitched in front of the finalist jury.

Challenge

We were provided historical heart rate and steps data taken from users of both the Apple Watch and FitBit. We were tasked to use this data to predict the onset of illness related to Covid-19 or similar viral infections before symptoms were reported by the user. This challenge was supported by AI4Medicine.
